9. show that the following equalities are incorrect:

(a). 10*n*^2^ + 9 = *O*(*n*)

Ans: 10*n*^2^ + 9 is not less than or equal to *c* for any constant *c* and all *n*. *n* $\geq$ *n*~0~.

(b). *n*^2^log *n* = $\Theta$(*n*^2^)

Ans: log *n* grow faster than *c*~1~,so *n*^2^log *n* $\neq$ *O*(*n*^2^), so *n*^2^log *n* $\neq$ $\Theta$(*n*^2^)
